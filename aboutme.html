About Me

I'm a student at Lambda SChool studying Web Developtment. I have a background in both technical training and design. I'm slowly killing my houseplants, one succulent at a time.

I have serious web skills that will be shown in my awesome portfolio. You should check out my GitHub. Hire me, you won't regret it. Unless you have houseplants; then, you might.

It's a good thing patience is one of my highly-employable team skills.
